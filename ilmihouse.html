Community & Arts

ilmihouse: Cultivating Creative Minds.

Client

ilmihouse

Year

2024

Services

Social Enterprise Branding, Literary Arts Identity

ilmihouse is a social enterprise targeted at self-development and in turn the development of the community at large. Created as a centre for literary and creative arts, it provides free access to a broad selection of books.

The platform supports a collaborative community of readers, writers and creative minds. It serves as a hub for the literary and creative industries, fostering a culture of knowledge-sharing and artistic expression.

The Design Challenge

The challenge was to create an identity that felt intellectually stimulating yet warm and accessible to a diverse community.
Balancing Edge & Elegance: How do you represent a "creative hub" that is both a quiet library and a vibrant community space?
Social Growth: Designing a brand that communicates the message of self-development and community upliftment without appearing preachy.
