Heritage & Preservation

HHC: Safeguarding Nigeria's Rich Legacy.

Client

History and Heritage Collections

Year

2024

Services

Brand Strategy, Digital Archiving, Visual Identity

HHC is a dedicated institution committed to safeguarding and promoting the rich historical heritage of Nigeria. Our mission is to collect, preserve, and disseminate historical documents, artifacts, and cultural expressions that tell the unique stories of the nation's past.

We strive to identify, digitize, catalog, and archive relevant materials from all regions of Nigeria, ensuring their accessibility to researchers, scholars, and the general public. Through our efforts, we aim to foster a deeper understanding of Nigerian history and contribute to the preservation of its cultural legacy for generations to come.

The Design Challenge

The core challenge was to create a visual identity that felt both deeply rooted in history and exceptionally modern.
Historical Honor: How do you represent centuries of diverse culture without relying on clichés or becoming visually cluttered?
Accessibility: Designing a system that feels inviting to the general public while maintaining the gravitas required for a historical institution.

The Design Solution

We developed a "Living Archive" concept—an identity that acts as a container for history rather than just a static symbol.
Modular Layouts: Creating a flexible grid system that can accommodate varying types of media, from 18th-century manuscripts to modern digital photography.
Sophisticated Palette: Using a refined color story that echoes the earthy tones of Nigerian artifacts while utilizing clean, minimalist typography to ensure a premium, timeless feel.
