History and Heritage
Collections

HHC is a dedicated institution committed to safeguarding and promoting the rich historical heritage of Nigeria. Our mission is to collect, preserve, and disseminate historical documents, artifacts, and cultural expressions that tell the unique stories of the nation's past. We strive to identify, digitize, catalog, and archive relevant materials from all regions of Nigeria, ensuring their accessibility to researchers, scholars, and the general public. Through our efforts, we aim to foster a deeper understanding of Nigerian history and contribute to the preservation of its cultural legacy for generations to come.
